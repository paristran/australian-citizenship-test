# Journey Themes Guide

## Available Themes

### 🎨 Aussie Green (Default)
- **Colors:** Green to Emerald gradient
- **Best for:** Traditional Australian theme
- **Accent:** Green timeline and buttons

### 🌅 Golden Sunset
- **Colors:** Orange to Amber gradient
- **Best for:** Warm, celebratory feeling
- **Accent:** Orange highlights

### 🌊 Ocean Blue
- **Colors:** Blue to Cyan gradient
- **Best for:** Fresh, modern look
- **Accent:** Blue accents

### 🏜️ Outback Red
- **Colors:** Red to Orange gradient
- **Best for:** Aboriginal-inspired earth tones
- **Accent:** Red highlights

### 🌼 Golden Wattle
- **Colors:** Yellow to Green gradient
- **Best for:** Australia's national flower theme
- **Accent:** Yellow/gold accents

### 🌃 Twilight Purple
- **Colors:** Purple to Pink gradient
- **Best for:** Unique, modern aesthetic
- **Accent:** Purple highlights

## How to Use

1. Navigate to `/journey`
2. Select your favorite theme from the grid
3. Add your milestones with dates
4. Click "Save Journey"
5. Preview updates automatically
6. Download PNG or share on social media

## Tips

- Themes are saved with your journey
- Try different themes for different sharing platforms
- Darker themes work better for printed images
- Lighter themes work better for social media posts
